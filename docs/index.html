Game Description

The game features an elderly person who is stuck in their old ways and refuses to coexist with the new technology and skyscrapers that are growing around them. Instead of moving to the retirement home, the player decides to take to the porch to prevent the delivery of anymore Shamazon packages.

Development Timeline

Development took place over the span of three weeks! During this short time, we were able to build an initial version of the game, perform user tests to get feedback, and then build a final version of the game based on that feedback. To develop our game, we used Unity and Unity's SteamVR plugin.

After our user testing, we found that players were not comfortable with how they had to hold the controllers. To address this, we gutted a Nerf gun and added one controller to be the grip and trigger of the gun and another controller was duct taped to the forend for reloading the gun. After changing the controller setup, users found the game easier to understand how to play and more enjoyable to play.
